# Movi

[ESP8266](https://docs.platformio.org/en/latest/boards/espressif8266/nodemcuv2.html) based Morse code/serial data transceiver and education tool.

Final Semester Project (FSP) of students of Tutorial Class E2T2 2025/2026, Kolej Matrikulasi Kejuruteraan Kedah (KMKK) under the guidance of Mr. Ahmad Hazeri bin Md Nor, Electrical & Electronics Engineering Lecturer.
